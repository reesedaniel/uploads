POTATOES

Informative Questions and Answers

Reese Daniel - 2013

What is a potato?

This is a potato: It looks kinda like a turd, but it really is delicious!
There are many different kinds of potatoes, but here are my favourites:

  • Yukon Gold
  • Russet
  • White
  • Yellow
  • Purple
  • Fingerling

What does it taste like?

Potatoes taste like heaven in your mouth: slightly sweet, fluffy on the inside and cruchy on the outside! Potatoes go with just about any other food (they even go well with other potatoes!). Dip them in ketchup! Dip them in mustard! Don't dip them at all! The fact of the matter is that potatoes are the greatest food on earth!

What's your favourite potato?

When it comes to potatoes, I don't pick favourites. Having said that, I do enjoy a good potato wedge covered in thyme and other herbs!
